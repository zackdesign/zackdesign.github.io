About Us

Zack Design specializes in delivering high-performance web applications and digital solutions for businesses of all sizes. Led by Isaac Rowntree, we provide industry-leading expertise in web development, consulting, and project management.

With over 20 years of experience in the tech industry, Isaac brings a wealth of knowledge from his roles as Head of Tech at CamperMate, Software Engineer at Redbubble, and Senior Developer at Ray White Group. His expertise spans:

  • Remote team leadership and engineering culture development
  • Full-stack development with React, TypeScript, and Node.js
